Apple Inc.

Apple Inc., established in 1976 by Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak, is a leading American technology company renowned for its innovative products and services. Headquartered in Cupertino, California, Apple has significantly influenced the personal computing and mobile industries.

Key Products and Services

  • Mac Computers: Since 1984, the Mac lineup has set standards in personal computing.
  • iPhone: Introduced in 2007, the iPhone revolutionized mobile technology, altering communication and media consumption.
  • iPad: Launched in 2010, the iPad bridged the gap between smartphones and laptops, creating a new market for digital tablets.
  • Apple Watch: A wearable device integrating health and fitness tracking with seamless connectivity to other Apple services.
  • Services: Apple offers a suite of services, including the App Store, Apple Music, iCloud, and Apple TV+, contributing significantly to its revenue.

Recent Developments

In the fourth quarter of 2024, Apple achieved record revenue of $94.9 billion, with iPhone sales increasing by 5.5% year-over-year to $46.2 billion. The company continues to innovate, introducing new products and services that resonate with consumers worldwide.
